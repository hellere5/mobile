Eliza Heller

My name is Eliza Heller and I am a student at the University of Michigan working to receive my BSI from the School of Information and a minor from the Ross School of Business. I have always been interested in cooking and eating culturally diverse foods and wanted to share my favoritie recipes with you!

I grew up in Cleveland, Ohio and have lived their my whole life. I went to Gilmour Academy for Highschool and played ice hockey and lacrosse. During my freetime, I enjoyed hanging out with my friends and cooking. I would always help my mom cook dinner for our family.

A little secret about me is that I love to travel. My family and I love taking trips together to different parts of the world. In this picture, we were in Tel Aviv have appetizers together while looking at the beautiful city. It's the little things in life that make you happy.

Another thing I love to do is hang out with my friends. In photo, my friends and I were hanging out on the beach and having a picnic. We were able to watch the sunset behind the water and of course were able to get good photos. This is something my friends and I love to do when it is nice outside. This photo was also taken on the beach in Tel Aviv.

Here is a photo of me and my family at my sister's graduation. My family and I love spending time together. I have 3 siblings, two of them are older and one of them is younger.

A fun fact about me is that I used to play ice hockey. I had played since I was 5 years old. My family has actually always been known as a hockey family becuase my entire family played hockey growing up. I played hockey all over world: in Prague, Italy, Canada, and Finland.

Another fun fact about me is that I played lacrosse in high school. I love being active and playing sports. Playing lacrosse in high school was so much fun and gave me so many lifelong friends!
